Awareness of Mental Illnesses and Disorders

What is Mental Health?
Mental Health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps and determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices.
What are mental illnesses OR disorders?
Mental Illness, as defined by WHO, is "a state of well-being in which the individual realizes his or her own abilities, can cope with the normal stresses of life, can work productively and fruitfully, and is able to make a contribution to his or her community"
